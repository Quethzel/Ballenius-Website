Your Task Manager

A simple way to handle your activities

Why we do it?

We believe that we don't need a toDo app with a lot of functionality and configurations, we need just a piece of paper with the versatility of the digital world. we believe that we can handle our daily activities without carrying a notebook. We want a simple way to know if what we do and what should be do it's the same.

How we do it?

People who works as a freelance, entrepeneure, creatives, home offices, incoworkers and digital nomadas as us need a way to stay in the right path (no procrestination). We start using Design Thinking and develop a simple task manager with special atention in "what task are the most important"

What expect?

  • Stay focus in the priority task
  • Check if what you did is that you had to do
  • Keep the thinkgs simple
